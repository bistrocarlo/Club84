Club 84 at Reggio Calabria Club – Master Plan

Club 84 becomes the new president & social club for Avondale Football Club, operating from the Reggio Calabria Club precinct (adjacent Avenger Park, Parkville). Open to members, players, officials, and the public on selected days. Fully funded and developed by Sportivo Industries.

Venue highlights (recap for committee)

13 match day games

Post‑match food service for opposition, senior men’s & women’s teams, and referees. Food prepared upstairs (pizza oven + ballroom kitchen) – finger food & antipasto platters.

Two soundproofed cocktail rooms

Doyle Street Room & AJ Davis Reserve – each holds 80–100 guests for nightclub/cocktail setup. Fully walled, soundproofed, separate from public bar.

Public bar open Saturdays, Sundays & match days – no membership required to watch sport and have a drink. Private rooms accessible only with Club 84 Relury Club Card (members of Reggio Calabria Club or Avondale FC).
