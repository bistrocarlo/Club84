Club 84 Build Completion

Checklist for Avondale FC & Reggio Calabria Club Committees · Tick off each item to finalise the super club

Colour scheme & nightclub atmosphere
  • Royal blue (#0055A4) as primary – approved and applied to all branding, furniture, and feature walls
  • Italian tricolor accents (green, white, red) – integrated into hero wall, bar front, or lighting strips
  • Nightclub lighting package – RGB LED wash lights, moving heads, disco/mood lighting, strobes, and dimmable zones
  • Surround sound system – zone-controlled, high-quality speakers in both private rooms and public bar/bistro
  • Fixtures & fittings – black ceilings, recessed LED strips, mirrored accents, illuminated bar counters
Memorabilia & media wall (public area)
  • Avondale Heritage Wall – request to Avondale FC for contribution of past shirts, trophies, flags, and historical items
  • Display cabinets & lighting – secure, illuminated showcases for memorabilia
  • Interactive media wall & red carpet platform – built on a raised platform with red carpet, branded backdrop, and professional lighting
  • Photo booth setup – automated camera station with club branding, instant social media posting capability (hashtag #Club84)
  • Children’s celebrity experience – kids can pose like soccer stars; photos shared to enhance Avondale’s public visibility
Avondale contribution requested: Provide heritage items and a financial / in‑kind contribution toward making the public area stylish and on‑brand.
Educational & soccer university use (weekday programs)
  • Children’s training programs (e.g., 80/20 with Moe) – use the soundproofed rooms for educational soccer sessions during weekdays/evenings
  • Parent waiting area – while children train, parents can wait in the public sports bar / bistro area, enjoying drinks and food
  • Soccer University for Avondale’s player regime – dedicated weekly sessions for Avondale’s entire senior and youth squads using the rooms as a classroom / tactical analysis hub
  • Equipment & technology – install whiteboards, video analysis screens, and Wi‑Fi for coaching presentations
  • Scheduling & booking system – integrate with Club 84’s existing reservation system to prioritise Avondale’s educational use
Benefit: Transforms Club 84 into a community hub for player development, keeping families engaged and driving bar/food revenue while children learn.
Membership plans & honor board
  • Life Member – $10,000 one‑time → 20% F&B discount, name on Life Members board
  • Foundation Member – $5,000 one‑time → 10% F&B discount, foundation recognition
  • Generic Club Member – annual fee → 10% F&B discount (Reggio Calabria or Avondale FC members)
  • Club 84 Relury Club Card – physical/digital card for access to private rooms and discounts
  • Honor Board installation – permanent display listing Presidents, Vice Presidents, Treasurers, and Life Members (inside restaurant)
  • Committee approval – sign‑off on membership tiers, pricing, and discount structure
Financial note: All revenue from Life, Foundation, and generic memberships goes directly to Avondale FC. Club 84 merely hosts the Honor Board on Avondale’s behalf.
Advisors & committee consultation
  • Joint advisory group formed – representatives from Avondale FC, Reggio Calabria Club, and Members Lounge
  • Regular consultation meetings – schedule monthly until build completion
  • Design & fit‑out approvals – both committees to sign off on final plans for public area (Avondale contribution) and internal area (Members Lounge funding)
Funding responsibilities
  • Public area (sports bar, bistro, heritage wall, media wall) – Avondale FC contribution requested to ensure stylish, on‑brand finish
  • Internal area (two soundproofed rooms, private bars, lighting, sound, furniture) – funded entirely by Members Lounge (current licensee at Reggio Calabria Club)
  • Match day games model – minimum 13 games, scalable to 36 based on bookings and minimum spend requirements
  • Junior birthday parties – 10% discount for Avondale junior players; operational during weeknights
  • Educational & soccer university sessions – no venue hire fee for Avondale’s official programs; bar/food sales from parents generate revenue
Revenue sharing (if any): Bar and food revenue from private rooms stays with Club 84 / Members Lounge; membership fees go to Avondale FC. Both committees to review and sign.
Final approvals to complete build
  • Avondale FC Committee – formal resolution to approve the master plan and contribution to public area
  • Reggio Calabria Club Committee – formal resolution to approve the hosted venue and internal funding by Members Lounge
  • Licensing & compliance – all necessary permits for soundproofing, extended trading, and public events
  • Build completion sign‑off – joint inspection and handover
Once all items are ticked, Club 84 will become a premier super club – honouring Avondale’s heritage, developing future players, and serving the community.
